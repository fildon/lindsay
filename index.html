Lindsay Meller

Lindsay was my mother. Lindsay was born in Te Awamutu, New Zealand, to Alfred and Fredda Duncalfe. Lindsay was mother to Tracey, Felix and Rupert. In 2017 Lindsay died to lung cancer.

I have aimed to order photos chronologically, name all pictured individuals, and include any written text on the back of each photo in the description. I have likely made some mistakes. Let me know if you can help me fill in blanks, or make corrections. Get in touch with me via email at maxfmckay@gmail.com
